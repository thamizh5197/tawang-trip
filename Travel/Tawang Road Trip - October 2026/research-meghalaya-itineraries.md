## Itinerary 1 — Meghalaya solo/hostel trip (11 days, Reddit)

**Vibe:** Solo traveler, hostel-based, budget-friendly, relaxed pace.

### Key takeaways

**Stays:**
- **Zostel Shillong** — great canteen food, table tennis, social scene, bonfire nights. Located downhill; city views if you climb uphill.
- **The Anchorage (Lachumiere)** — Airbnb, used as Shillong base after Zostel.
- **Aïom Sohra (Cherrapunjee)** — comfy, homely Airbnb. Househelp cooked great home food.
- **Maple Pine (Mawphlang)** — unique isolated stay, no WiFi (Jio works). Amazing pancakes. Highly recommended by another Redditor.

**Food:**
- **Dylan's Cafe (Shillong)** — highly recommended for brunch.
- **Darimi Kitchen (Cherrapunjee area)** — good food.
- **Ward's Lake night market** — millet-based food stall (cookies, cheesecake — delicious).
- **Maple Pine pancakes** — mentioned twice, clearly memorable.

**Experiences rated by this traveler:**
- **Nongjrong sunrise** — stunning (left at 3am to catch it).
- **Double Decker Root Bridge trek** — worth it. Started 10am from Tyrna, no guide needed, back by 4pm.
- **Rainbow Falls** — breathtaking but exhausting.
- **Sacred Forest Mawphlang** — worth visiting (₹200 pick & drop from Maple Pine).
- **Mawsmai Cave** — "worth it" (contradicts our user's claustrophobia note).
- **Dawki** — felt like a tourist trap after 3h one-way drive.
- **Mawlynnong** — tourist trap. Single-decker root bridge was "meh." Local food unimpressive.
- **7 Sisters Falls** — no water (confirms our "often dry in October" note).
- **Ward's Lake** — night market was fun, lake itself average.
- **Hill Mount School volunteering** — very fulfilling.

**Practical notes:**
- Guwahati → Shillong shared taxi: ₹800–1,000.
- Dawki + Mawlynnong day cab: ₹3,700 (split 3 ways).
- Shillong → Cherrapunjee cab: ₹1,500.
- Mawphlang → Shillong cab: ₹1,000.
- Shillong → airport shared taxi: ₹1,000 (but got stuck in traffic, paid ₹4,500 for alternate route).
- Nongriat homestays available but no internet.
- Activa rental available in Cherrapunjee area.

**New place to consider:**
- **Nongjrong** — sunrise viewpoint, stunning. Not in our current plan.
- **Maple Pine Mawphlang** — unique stay experience.
- **Sacred Forest** — was in our "dropped" list but this traveler found it worthwhile.

## Itinerary 2 — Meghalaya 7-day trip (Dec, Google Sheet)

**Vibe:** 7 days, Delhi-based, mix of hostels/resorts/homestays. ₹42,200 total.

### Key takeaways

**Stays:**
- **Zostel Shillong** — ₹1,200/night, rated 4/5.
- **Sai Mika Resort (Cherrapunjee)** — tent upgrade available.
- **Kupli Orchid Resort** — ₹1,600/night.
- **Nongjrong Village homestay** — ₹500/night camping. Friendly owner, exceptional views.
- **April Springs BNB (Shillong)** — ₹1,300/night, rated 5/5.

**Experiences rated by this traveler:**
- **Wah Japuh (Mawlong Village)** — "hidden gem", swimming + cliff jumping, 5/5. Near Cherrapunjee.
- **Krem Chympe cave** — "highly recommended, difficult but doable trek." 4x4 access. Near Kupli area.
- **Nongjrong Village sunrise** — rated well, ₹50 viewpoint fee.
- **Pung Iyer Falls** — 4.5/5, "offbeat, less crowded."
- **Wei Sadong Falls** — visited same day as Pung Iyer.
- **Khaddum Falls** — swimming spot near Chympe Village.
- **Kupli River speed boat** — ₹670.
- **Laitlum Canyon** — rated 2/5, "underwhelming, skipable." (Contradicts Itinerary 1 and our plan — may depend on weather/time of day.)
- **Umiam Lake boating** — 3/5.
- **Police Bazaar** — 3/5.

**New places to consider:**
- **Wah Japuh (Mawlong Village)** — hidden gem swimming + cliff jumping, not in our plan.
- **Krem Chympe cave** — different from Mawsmai, more adventurous.
- **Pung Iyer Falls & Wei Sadong Falls** — offbeat waterfalls near Cherrapunjee.
- **Kupli River** — speed boat experience.
- **Nongjrong Village** — confirmed great sunrise spot (also in Itinerary 1).

**Practical notes:**
- Guwahati → Shillong cab: ₹7,300 (seems high — may be private/premium).
- Total trip cost: ₹42,200 for 7 days (flights included from Delhi).

## Itinerary 3 — Tawang Circuit 9-day (theroamingshoes.com blog)

**Vibe:** Detailed Tawang-focused circuit. Oct–Nov recommended. 9 days.

### Key takeaways

**Route:** Guwahati → Shergaon → Dirang → Tawang → Bomdila → Guwahati.

**New places to consider:**

- **Shergaon** — stopover between Guwahati and Dirang. Lower Monastery (butter tea), Zengbu Gompa (400 yrs old, hilltop), Choskorong Waterfall (30-min hidden gem hike), Buddha Park in Mushaksing village.
- **TDL Monastery (Dirang)** — architectural marvel with café, overlooks valley. Not in our plan.
- **Khinmey Monastery (Tawang)** — colorful Medicine Buddha interior. Not in our plan.
- **Urgelling Gompa** — 600-year-old birthplace of 6th Dalai Lama, meditative. Not in our plan.
- **Tawang War Memorial Light & Sound show** — ₹50, sunset views of monastery. Not in our plan.
- **Chillipam & Lumum Falls** — optional stops on Bomdila → Guwahati return leg.
- **Nameri National Park** — on the scenic route via Tezpur/Bhalukpong.

**Confirms our existing plan:**
- Sela Pass, Sela Lake, Jaswant Garh, Nuranang Falls — all validated.
- Mandala Top (108 stupas) — worth it.
- Sangti Valley — recommended, plus Government Sheep Farm at sunset.
- Bumla Pass — local taxi required, 19+ lakes on the route, army briefing.
- PTSO Lake — afternoon reflections best.
- Madhuri Lake — dead tree trunks in water, unique.
- Chakzam Bridge — 700 yrs old, bamboo-based iron suspension bridge. Confirmed worth visiting.
- Giant Buddha Statue — quick stop near monastery.

**Tawang additions for our extra day (Day 7):**
- Urgelling Gompa (birthplace of 6th Dalai Lama)
- Khinmey Monastery
- War Memorial Light & Sound show at sunset
- Chakzam Bridge (already noted from video)

**Route options Guwahati → Arunachal:**
- Route 1 (Kalaktang): 250 km, 7h — this is what our plan uses.
- Route 2 (Tezpur/Bhalukpong): 350 km, 8h — scenic, passes Nameri NP, Tippi & Lumum waterfalls.

**Practical notes:**
- Dirang to Tawang: 4h (6-7h with stops).
- Tawang to Bomdila: 5h.
- Best season: Oct to early Nov for clear skies.
- Author recommends extra time in Dirang for Lubrang/Thembang village exploration.
- Jang Falls: visit before 3:30 PM for rainbow.

## Itinerary 4 — Meghalaya 5-day adventure tour

**Vibe:** Packed adventure itinerary, 5 days, activity-heavy. Likely a tour package.

### Key takeaways

**Experiences rated/mentioned:**
- **Nohkalikai Falls** — tallest plunge waterfall in India. Near Cherrapunjee.
- **Wei Saw Dong Falls** — three-tiered waterfall. (Also mentioned in Itinerary 2 as good.)
- **Prut Falls** — near Cherrapunjee, start-of-day stop.
- **Bamboo Trek** — near Cherrapunjee, followed by waterfall dip.
- **Krang Suri Falls** — "magnificent", with zipline over the falls. Near Jowai/Dawki area.
- **Kayaking + cliff jumping at Dawki** — crystal-clear river, but rain affects visibility.
- **Sacred Forest** — included here (3rd itinerary to mention it).
- **Elephant Falls** — included but we've already dropped it.
- **Umiam Lake** — en route Guwahati→Shillong, quick stop.
- **Mawsmai Cave** — included (user is claustrophobic though).
- **Seven Sister Falls** — included but often dry in October.

**New places to consider:**
- **Krang Suri Falls + zipline** — adventure activity, near Dawki/Jowai area. Was in our "would add with more time" list. Multiple itineraries now mention it.
- **Nohkalikai Falls** — India's tallest plunge waterfall, not in our plan. Near Cherrapunjee.
- **Bamboo Trek** — could be the Mawryngkhang bamboo bridge trek (was in "would add" list).

**Confirms:**
- Dawki/riverside camping with kayaking + cliff jumping — validates Shnongpdeng plan.
- Sacred Forest keeps showing up across itineraries (3 out of 4 now).
- Wei Saw Dong Falls mentioned in multiple itineraries — offbeat, worth considering.
