# Meghalaya Stopover (pre-Tawang)

> Duration: **4 days / 3 nights** (Oct 16–19)
> Focus: experiences + adventure, no tourist traps
> Last updated: 2026-06-28

---

## 4-Day Itinerary

### Day 1 — Oct 16 (Fri) · Laitlum Canyon + Shillong
Fly Chennai → Guwahati → drive to **Laitlum Canyon** ("End of Hills", 45 km from Shillong). 360° valley views, clouds below you. Afternoon light is best. Continue to Shillong.
**Overnight: Shillong.**

### Day 2 — Oct 17 (Sat) · Nongriat Trek
Shillong → Tyrna → trek 3,500 steps down to **Nongriat village**. Visit **Double Decker Living Root Bridge** + **Rainbow Falls** (skip first lagoon, blue lagoon later is better). Stay overnight in the village — don't do the brutal same-day return.
**Overnight: Nongriat village.**

### Day 3 — Oct 18 (Sun) · Trek Up + Shnongpdeng
Early morning climb back up (2.5h). Drive ~3h to **Shnongpdeng** on the Umngot River. Crystal-clear water, riverside camping.
**Overnight: Shnongpdeng (camping ~₹400/head).**

### Day 4 — Oct 19 (Mon) · Freedive + Head to Tawang
Morning **freediving** in the Umngot (15m visibility, emerald-green water). Then drive to Guwahati (3h), pick up bikes, ride Kalaktang route to Bomdila (7h). Long day.

---

## About Shnongpdeng

- **Location:** West Jaintia Hills, ~90 km from Shillong via Dawki (~3h drive). 2 km from Dawki but far quieter. Near India-Bangladesh border.
- **The river:** Umngot River — boats appear to float on air. 15m visibility in October.
- **Activities:** Freediving, snorkeling, scuba (₹3,500), cliff jumping, kayaking, boating.
- **Best time:** Oct–Feb (water clearest).
- **Why not Dawki:** Same river, but Dawki is overcrowded and noisy. Shnongpdeng has a fraction of the crowds.
- **Camping:** Riverside camping from ~₹400/head. Book ahead for October.

## Dropped (with reasons)

- **Mawsmai Cave** — generic tourist cave, user is claustrophobic
- **Sacred Forest Mawphlang** — good but not top-tier, created time pressure
- **Dawki** — overcrowded; Shnongpdeng is the same river without the circus
- **Mawlynnong** — overrated, overly commercialized "cleanest village" tag
- **Seven Sisters Falls** — often dry in October
- **Ward's Lake** — average
- **Elephant Falls** — generic, overcrowded

## Would add with more time
- **Kongthong whistling village** — unique cultural experience (+half day)
- **Krang Suri Falls** — "most beautiful waterfall in India" near Jowai (+half day)
- **Mawryngkhang Trek** — bamboo bridges over gorges, scariest trek in Meghalaya (+half day)
