Meghalaya · Days 1–4
1
Oct 16 · Friday
Fly In + Night Drive to Shillong
Fly Chennai 14:40 → Guwahati 17:45. Grab dinner near the airport, then night drive to Shillong (~3h, arrive ~21:30). Check in and crash early — big day tomorrow.
3h flight + 3h drive stay: shillong
2
Oct 17 · Saturday
Sunrise Laitlum + Nongriat Trek
Wake early, drive to Laitlum Canyon (~45 km from Shillong) — "End of Hills." 360° views of endless valleys with mist in the morning light. Spend ~1.5h. Then drive to Tyrna village (~2.5h). Trek 3,500 steps down through jungle to Nongriat village. Visit the Double Decker Living Root Bridge — the only one in the world. Hike to Rainbow Falls (skip first lagoon, the blue lagoon later is better). Stay overnight in the village.
1h drive + 1.5h canyon + 2.5h drive + 1.5h descent packed day · leave by 5:30 AM laitlum canyon root bridge rainbow falls stay: nongriat village
3
Oct 18 · Sunday
Trek Up + Shnongpdeng Riverside Camp
Early morning climb back up from Nongriat (3,500 steps, ~2.5h). Drive to Shnongpdeng on the crystal-clear Umngot River. Set up riverside camp. Evening by the water.
2.5h climb + 3h drive riverside camping stay: shnongpdeng camp

Shnongpdeng — The Spot

Same crystal-clear Umngot River as Dawki but without the tourist circus. Boats appear to float on air. 15m underwater visibility in October.

Location
West Jaintia Hills, ~90 km from Shillong, near India-Bangladesh border
How to reach
Shillong → Dawki road (3h), take Shnongpdeng turn before Dawki town
Activities
Freediving, snorkeling, cliff jumping, kayaking, boating
Best time
Oct–Feb (clearest water, emerald-green)
Camping
Riverside spots from ~₹400/head
4
Oct 19 · Monday
Freedive + Drive to Bomdila
Morning freediving in the Umngot — 15m visibility, transparent emerald-green water. Then drive to Guwahati, pick up the rental car, and drive the Kalaktang route to Bomdila. Passes Zangdokpalri Monastery en route.
freediving 3h drive + 7h drive long day stay: bomdila
Tawang Circuit · Days 5–9
5
Oct 20 · Tuesday · Vijaya Dasami
Bomdila → Dirang — Acclimatize
Short drive to Dirang. Rest and acclimatize before the high passes. Explore Sangti Valley (riverside meadows, orchards), soak in the hot springs, visit Mandala Top (108 stupas in concentric circles).
1.5h drive + local sangti valley hot springs mandala top stay: dirang
6
Oct 21 · Wednesday
Dirang → Tawang via Sela Pass
The highlight drive. Cross Sela Pass (13,700 ft) — frozen lake, war memorial, army tea canteen. The 2024 Sela Tunnel keeps the route open even in snow. Cross before noon as winds and fog roll in. Stop at Jaswant Garh War Memorial and Nuranang (Jang) Falls (100m cascade).
~6h drive · 135 km sela pass 13,700 ft nuranang falls stay: tawang
7
Oct 22 · Thursday
Tawang Monastery + Town Exploration
Pre-dawn Tawang Monastery for morning prayers (largest in India, 2nd largest in Asia). Then explore at your own pace: Urgelling Gompa (600-year-old birthplace of the 6th Dalai Lama), Khinmey Monastery (colorful Medicine Buddha interior), Chakzam Bridge (700-year-old iron suspension bridge), Giant Buddha Statue. Evening: Tawang War Memorial Light & Sound show at sunset (₹50) — views of the monastery across the valley.
relaxed day · local monastery sunrise urgelling gompa chakzam bridge war memorial stay: tawang
8
Oct 23 · Friday
Bumla Pass + PTSO Lake + Madhuri Lake
Full day excursion by local taxi. Drive to Bumla Pass (15,200 ft) — Indo-China border, army briefing, 19+ lakes along the route. Stop at PTSO Lake (travelers say more impressive than Madhuri — afternoon reflections are best) and Madhuri Lake (Sangetsar Tso) with its haunting dead tree trunks.
full day · taxi ~₹5,500 bumla 15,200 ft ptso lake madhuri lake stay: tawang
9
Oct 24 · Saturday
Tawang → Bomdila
Begin the descent. Recross Sela Pass early morning, slower pace, more photo stops. Visit Bomdila Monastery.
6–7h drive stay: bomdila

Essentials

Cash

Load up at Guwahati & Bomdila. ATMs past Bomdila are unreliable and run dry. Budget ₹1,500–3,000/day.

Connectivity

BSNL and Airtel work patchily. Jio unreliable. Download offline maps before leaving Shillong.

Altitude

Acclimatize in Dirang (Day 5). Carry Diamox if advised. Sela is 13,700 ft, Bumla is 15,200 ft.

Gear

Thermals, windproof jacket, waterproofs, grip shoes, woolen gloves. Fashion coats are useless at 13,000+ ft.

Fuel

Fill up at Bomdila — stations are scarce beyond. Carry a small reserve can. No fuel between Bomdila and Tawang.

Food

Army-canteen Maggi & tea on high stretches (Sela, Bumla). Carry snacks and water for ride days.

Medical

Personal kit with altitude meds (Diamox if advised), painkillers, prescriptions. Nearest major hospital is a long drive away.
