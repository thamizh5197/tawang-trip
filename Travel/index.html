Shnongpdeng — The Spot

Same crystal-clear Umngot River as Dawki but without the tourist circus. Boats appear to float on air. 15m underwater visibility in October.

Location
West Jaintia Hills, ~90 km from Shillong, near India-Bangladesh border
How to reach
Shillong → Dawki road (3h), take Shnongpdeng turn before Dawki town
Activities
Freediving, snorkeling, cliff jumping, kayaking, upstream forest walks, nearby root bridge
Best time
Oct–Feb (clearest water, emerald-green)
Camping
Riverside spots from ~₹800/person

Essentials

Cash

Load up at Guwahati & Bomdila. ATMs past Bomdila are unreliable and run dry. Nongriat has no ATM or UPI. Budget ₹1,500–3,000/day.

Connectivity

BSNL and Airtel work patchily. Jio unreliable. Download offline maps before leaving Shillong.

Altitude

Acclimatize in Dirang (Day 5). Carry Diamox if advised. Sela is 13,700 ft, Bumla is 15,200 ft.

Gear

Thermals, windproof jacket, waterproofs, grip shoes, woolen gloves. Fashion coats are useless at 13,000+ ft.

Fuel

Fill up at Bomdila — stations are scarce beyond. Carry a small reserve can. No fuel between Bomdila and Tawang.

Food

Army-canteen Maggi & tea on high stretches (Sela, Bumla). Carry snacks and water for ride days.

Medical

Personal kit with altitude meds (Diamox if advised), painkillers, prescriptions. Nearest major hospital is a long drive away.
